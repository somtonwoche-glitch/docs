# Payment Methods

StudyOps uses **Paystack** for secure payments in Naira (₦).

## Accepted Methods
- Debit/Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
- Bank transfer
- USSD

## Pricing
- **Free tier** — Basic features, limited daily AI queries
- **Premium** — Full access, voice features, unlimited queries from ₦2,000/month

All payments are processed securely through Paystack. No hidden charges.
