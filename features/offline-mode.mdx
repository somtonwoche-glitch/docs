# Offline Mode

StudyOps saves your conversations, quizzes, and study materials locally. You can review them even without an internet connection.

## What Works Offline
- Previous chat conversations
- Completed quizzes and results
- Study plans and schedules
- Uploaded documents (cached)

## What Needs Internet
- New AI conversations
- Generating new quizzes
- Voice features
- File uploads
