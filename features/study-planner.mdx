# Study Planner

Get a personalized study schedule based on your courses and exam dates.

## How It Works
1. Go to the **Planner** tab
2. Enter your courses and exam dates
3. AI generates a day-by-day study plan
4. Track your progress as you study

The planner considers topic difficulty, your available time, and spaced repetition principles.
