# Lecture Recording

Record your lectures in-app and get instant AI-powered transcriptions and summaries.

## How It Works
1. Click the **microphone** icon during a lecture
2. StudyOps records the audio
3. AI transcribes the lecture (powered by OpenAI Whisper)
4. Ask Sage questions about the lecture content
5. Generate study notes from the recording
