# Sage AI Chat

The core of StudyOps. Ask Sage anything academic and get clear, exam-focused explanations.

## Smart Routing
Sage automatically picks the best AI for your question — Claude for explanations, DeepSeek for math, Perplexity for research, GPT-4 for images.

## Features
- **Exam-focused answers** — Responses tailored to WAEC, JAMB, NECO, and university exams
- **Follow-up questions** — Ask "explain more" or "give an example" to go deeper
- **Pidgin mode** — Get explanations in Nigerian Pidgin English
- **Verify answers** — Click Verify to fact-check any response with Perplexity
- **Voice input** — Speak your questions instead of typing
- **Image support** — Upload photos of questions or diagrams
