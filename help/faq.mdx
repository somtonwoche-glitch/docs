# Frequently Asked Questions

**Is StudyOps free?**
Yes! There's a free tier with basic features. Premium starts at ₦2,000/month.

**Does it work offline?**
Partially. You can review saved conversations and quizzes offline, but new AI responses need internet.

**What subjects does it cover?**
All subjects — Sciences, Arts, Social Sciences, Engineering, Medicine, Law, and more.

**Can I use it on my phone?**
Yes. StudyOps works on any device with a web browser — phone, tablet, or laptop.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. We use Firebase for authentication and don't share your data with third parties.

**Does it work on slow internet?**
Yes. StudyOps automatically detects your network speed and adapts responses accordingly.
