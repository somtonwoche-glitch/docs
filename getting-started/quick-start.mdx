# Quick Start Guide

## Step 1: Open StudyOps
Go to [rnpathfinder.netlify.app](https://rnpathfinder.netlify.app) on any device.

## Step 2: Sign Up
- **Google** — One-click sign in
- **Email & Password** — Create an account

## Step 3: Choose Your Personality
Pick how Sage talks to you: Friendly, Professional, Pidgin, or Strict Teacher.

## Step 4: Start Studying!
Type any question in the chat. Try: "Explain photosynthesis for JAMB"
