# Meet Sage

Sage is your personal AI tutor. It uses **6 AI models** working together:

| Question Type | AI Used | Why |
|---|---|---|
| General study questions | **Claude** | Best at explaining concepts |
| Math & calculations | **DeepSeek** | STEM reasoning specialist |
| Quick factual answers | **Groq** | Ultra-fast responses |
| Research & current info | **Perplexity** | Searches the internet |
| Image/PDF analysis | **GPT-4 Vision** | Reads images and documents |
| Voice responses | **ElevenLabs** | Natural voice (Premium) |

Sage picks the right AI automatically based on what you ask.
